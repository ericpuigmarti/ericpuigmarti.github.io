Eric is a Product Designer from Toronto, currently building digital products at FreshBooks.

With an obsession for well-designed products and a relentless attention to detail, Eric is passionate about designing and building meaningful products. It's what gets him up in the morning (and also ☕).

After shifting his passion and creativity from music to technology, Eric studied Art & Design to help bring creative ideas to life by building digital experience that have positive impact on the lives of others. Ever since, he has crafted a variety of web and mobile experiences working with small startups and established companies.

When he's not designing, he can be found hanging out with playing with his dog Bagel, or working on a few personal side projects.
